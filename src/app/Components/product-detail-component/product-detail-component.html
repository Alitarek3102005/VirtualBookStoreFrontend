The Silent
Echo

by Elena Rostov

Sci-Fi & Thriller
★★★★★ (4.2)

In a future where humanity has colonized the distant edges of the galaxy, communication between star systems relies on a network of ancient, alien relays. When the central hub suddenly goes dark, a lone engineer named Kaelen must navigate the silent void to uncover the truth.


Elena Rostov weaves a masterful tale of isolation, artificial intelligence, and the enduring human spirit in this award-winning novel that asks: what happens when the universe stops listening?
